The purpose of this project was to compare various
weather conditions in a variety of cities around the world.
Several aspects of the weather, such as wind speed, cloudiness,
and temperature, were compared based on the latitude of each city. The data was analyzed through Python using both the Pandas
and Matplotlib libraries. Statistics were also calculated
to draw conclusions for each comparison.
